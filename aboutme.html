About Me

I'm a junior software engineer with a passion for bringing ideas to life through code. I hold a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering from Southern New Hampshire University, where I graduated summa cum laude with a GPA of 3.99.

What truly drives me in my software engineering career is the thrill of creating something new. There's nothing quite like the excitement of turning an idea into reality and watching it grow and evolve. I thrive on the challenges and opportunities that the ever-evolving tech industry offers.
